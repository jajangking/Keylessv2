PEDOMAN WIRING KEYLESS SYSTEM ESP32
===================================

POWER SUPPLY:
VCC LED bar ───> ESP32 3V3
GND LED bar ───> ESP32 GND

LED INDICATOR:
LED1 ───> GPIO 4   (Indikator Siap)
LED2 ───> GPIO 5   (Indikator BT)
LED3 ───> GPIO 18  (Indikator WiFi)
LED4 ───> GPIO 19  (Kontak ON)
LED5 ───> GPIO 21  (Standar Naik)
LED6 ───> GPIO 22  (Tombol Dipencet Kontak ON - 4 Pin Button)
LED7 ───> GPIO 23  (Tombol Bypass)
LED8 ───> GPIO 25  (Mode Tidur)

INPUT BUTTON:
Button pin 1 ───> GPIO 13  (Main Button)
Button pin 2 ───> GND

ADDITIONAL 4-PIN BUTTON WITH LED:
Button pin 1 ───> GPIO 14  (Additional Button)
Button pin 2 ───> GND
LED Anode ───> GPIO 12  (Additional Button LED)
LED Cathode ───> GND  (through 220-330Ω resistor)

BUZZER:
Buzzer + ───> GPIO 15
Buzzer – ───> GND

RELAY OUTPUT:
Relay CH1 ───> GPIO 26 (Kontak - ON/OFF Switch)
Relay CH2 ───> GPIO 27 (Starter - Momentary Switch)

CATATAN:
- Pastikan polaritas LED benar (Anoda ke GPIO, Katoda ke GND melalui resistor jika diperlukan)
- Gunakan resistor pull-up/internal untuk button
- Jika menggunakan LED eksternal tanpa resistor, tambahkan resistor seri 220-330 ohm
- Sambungkan GND bersama antara ESP32, LED bar, button, dan relay module
- Relay digunakan untuk mengontrol sirkuit kontak dan starter secara aman
- Pastikan menggunakan relay module dengan driver IC (misalnya ULN2003) untuk proteksi GPIO
